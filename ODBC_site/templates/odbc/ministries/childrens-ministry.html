Childrens Ministry

ODBC Bible Club

Our Children’s Bible Clubs introduce children to Jesus through engaging activities, songs, and prayer. These activities are a great way to bring children together and allow leaders to build relationships with them.

Jasmine Street Kids for Jesus Bible Club

On the last Saturday of March 2011, a new initiative had begun. Mrs Yvonne Vernon (a member at ODBC) had always felt a burden on her heart to deliver the word of God to the kids in her neighbourhood and so, at 3:00 pm that day, the first bible club was held with about 23 children in attendance.

The format of the bible club is simple. The Children will sing a few songs and play a game and then most importantly, they are presented with God’s word in the form of a bible story or lesson as well as a memory verse. Bible club ends in the same fashion as it begins, with a prayer, making every effort to teach the children reverence for God Children are also given a snack at the end of each session. Knowing that the word of God shall not return void (Isaiah 55:11) the children are encouraged to remember the bible verse and are even given a small incentive for reciting the verse from the previous week.

At JSK for Jesus we also reach into the homes of these children by sending occasional donations of rice, beans and sugar which all come to us by donation from Mr. Paul Dycke of Blue Creek. This year we have begun bible club with a new vision in mind – taking the word of God into the homes of children. So in addition to snacks, and food stuff, we have begun to send home bible-based literature along with the kids, courtesy Belize Camping experience.

We have grown to having anything from 25 – 50 children on a weekly bases and we covet your prayers as we reach those kids in the Jasmine Street and ODBC Club for Jesus.

Helpers: Stella Codd, Alison Gutierrez, Irvin Vernon
