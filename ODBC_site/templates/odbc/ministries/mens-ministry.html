Mens Ministry

Our monthly men’s ministry is held on the Friday preceding Lord’s Supper at 7:00pm. This meeting is our disciple training program. Although it is for men, this ministry is a family effort in every way, as women and wives prepare a hearty meal and a snack. Our men’s ministry is considered to be one of the most important of all ministries; therefore, all male members are expected to attend.

Each 3-hour session is divided into sections that clearly define its purpose in fulfilling vital learning experiences for men:

1. For Fellowship

2. To Foster:
a. Spontaneous discussion
b. Answer question
c. And encourage shared experiences, and

3. To Facilitate Biblical teaching on
a. Relational matters
b. Communal matters
c. Doctrinal matters

This ministry is headed by our highly trained and experienced leadership team of Elders.
