Baptism Ministry

The Open Door Believers Chapel baptismal class comprises of 5-6 weeks of teaching and interactive sessions that seek to:

• Explain the basics of the Christian faith to persons who profess faith in Christ Jesus and who would like to demonstrate this faith through the act of baptism

• Make clear to those who are not sure, what it means to be saved by Christ.

The classes generally take place on an annual basis between the months of August and September. The following topics are dealt with each week:

What is Salvation? The story of our Redemption

The Christian Test 101 - (from Chuck Swindol)

Is baptism necessary?

What is the Lords Supper?

An overview of the Church – Where do I fit in?

How to tell your story/testimony
