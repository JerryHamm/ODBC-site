As leaders of God’s Girls Club, we try to exemplify and live out the command of God given in Titus 2 to teach the younger women what is good.

At ODBC, we have many young girls and older girls in need of Godly guidance, mentoring, and practical everyday teaching. About twenty five girls meet the first Saturday of each month for different sessions and activities.

As leaders, our hearts yearn to see each young girl grow in the wisdom and love of Jesus. We want to see each young girl be a woman of noble character, a woman who is faithful, a woman who can provide for her family – a Godly woman.

Pray with us as we work for His glory.
