Statement of Faith for Governance of the Church

We believe the Bible is God's Word, without error of any kind. It is accurate, authoritative and applicable to our everyday lives.

We believe every human being is born a sinner, in need of Salvation which comes through Jesus Christ alone, who save us from our sin.

We believe the New Birth or Born Again experience is the only way to qualify for Heaven.

We believe Salvation is possible in this life alone, and it is only through Faith in Christ not works, that we can be saved. We believe that only Jesus can forgive sin.

We believe in the security of the believer in Christ.

We believe that the True Church is comprised of Born Again Persons only.

We believe that there is One God in three Persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

We believe Jesus Christ is God who became human, we believe the Holy Spirit Save, Seals, Sanctifies, Equips, Enables and Encourages the believers in Christ.

We believe once truly saved, then saved.

We believe Jusus is coming for His True Church anytime soon (The Rapture).

We believe Jusus is coming WITH His Church to establish His Millennial Kingdom.

Open Doors Believers Chapel is not a pastor centered church, we are governed by a body of elders.
