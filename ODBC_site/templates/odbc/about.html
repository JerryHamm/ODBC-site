About us

Our Pledge

We pledge, in the name of Jesus Christ our Lord, with God as our Helper, and the Holy Spirit as our guide,

TO encourage the WORSHIP of GOD in the beauty of holiness.

TO honour and study the WORD of GOD in fear and openness.

TO maintain the WITNESS of GOD in all righteousness, and

TO seek the WILL of GOD for maximum effectiveness.

Statement of Faith for Governance of the Church

We believe the Bible is God's Word, without error of any kind. It is accurate, authoritative and applicable to our everyday lives. We believe every human being is born a sinner, in need of Salvation which comes through Jesus Christ alone, who save us from our sin.

We believe the New Birth or Born Again experience is the only way to qualify for Heaven. We believe Salvation is possible in this life alone, and it is only through Faith in Christ not works, that we can be saved. We believe that only Jesus can forgive sin. We believe in the security of the believer in Christ. We believe that the True Church is comprised of Born Again Persons only. We believe that there is One God in three Persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

We believe Jesus Christ is God who became human, we believe the Holy Spirit Saves, Seals, Sanctifies, equips, enables and encourages the believers in Christ. We believe once truly saved, then saved. We believe Jesus is coming for His True Church anytime soon (The Rapture). We believe Jesus is coming WITH His Church to establish His Millennial Kingdom

Our Ministries
